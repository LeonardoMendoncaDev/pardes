## Why PARDES

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="Intent first" icon="star">

	</Card>
	<Card title="Fifteen linked steps" icon="list-format">
		From Purpose to Evolution, fifteen steps alternating spec and execution,

	<Card title="Applied hermeneutics" icon="open-book">
		PaRDeS — Peshat, Remez, Drash, Sod. Four layers of reading
